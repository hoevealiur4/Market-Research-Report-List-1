<p><ul><li>Cloth</li><li>Tape</li><li>Twisted Rope</li><li>Yarn</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic fiber textiles, including cloth, tape, twisted rope, and yarn, are high-temperature-resistant materials used in industrial applications. Cloth is a flexible, woven fabric ideal for insulation and gaskets. Tape, a narrow strip of cloth, is used for wrapping and sealing. Twisted rope, made from braided fibers, provides strength and durability for sealing and jointing in high-heat environments. Yarn, a continuous strand of fibers, is used for weaving or sewing in thermal insulation. All types offer excellent thermal stability, chemical resistance, and low thermal conductivity.</p></p>
