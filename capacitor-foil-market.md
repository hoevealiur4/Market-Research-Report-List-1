<p><p>The capacitor foil market includes cathode and anode foils, essential components in electrolytic capacitors. Cathode foil, typically made of aluminum, serves as the negative electrode, providing a conductive surface for the electrolyte. Anode foil, also aluminum-based, acts as the positive electrode, featuring an etched surface to increase surface area and capacitance. Both foils are critical for energy storage, with anode foil requiring higher purity and specialized processing. The market is driven by demand in electronics, automotive, and renewable energy sectors.</p></p>
