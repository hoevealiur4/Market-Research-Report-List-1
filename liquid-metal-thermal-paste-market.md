<p><p>Liquid metal thermal paste is a high-performance thermal interface material designed to enhance thermal conductivity between heat-generating components, such as CPUs and GPUs, and their heatsinks. Unlike traditional thermal pastes, which are often silicone or polymer-based, liquid metal consists primarily of metallic alloys, offering superior thermal transfer properties. This makes it particularly suited for high-performance computing applications, including gaming, overclocking, and in industries that require effective heat dissipation.</p><p>The Liquid Metal Thermal Paste Market is experiencing significant growth, with a projected compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 5.9% during the forecast period.
